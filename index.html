About

Research Profile

I am a PhD researcher specialising in Structural Health Monitoring (SHM) using embedded piezoelectric transducers in concrete structures. My doctoral work focuses on developing temperature compensation methods and damage detection algorithms based on coda wave interferometry and dynamic time warping (DTW).

I have contributed to the European project USES2 (Novel Ultrasonic and Seismic methods) and performed experimental bridge monitoring at the Chevire Bridge in Nantes. I bring hands-on experience with laser Doppler vibrometry, closed-loop control testing, and bolted joint analysis.

I am actively seeking postdoctoral positions in SHM, ultrasonic NDE, or related signal processing research, where I can contribute to next-generation infrastructure monitoring systems.
